About Us


Through this website farmers or customers can reach out to other farmers to purchase the goods which are not readily available in the market. These goods include natural fertilizers,straw for feeding cattle,other seasonal plants etc. The farmers can connect to our service boy for selling their product. Buyers will create an account on the organic e-commerce platform, add products to the cart, then make payment for their order.The platform also has customization options , which helps both farmers and buyers to choose their convenient language,theme etc. The merchandise will be delivered to the customers location by delivery personnel. Farmers can either send the product straight to buyers after a sale or seek assistance from businesses to carry the e-Commerce organic products online from the farm to the final buyer. Product shipment can be handled by either the operator or a third-party logistic provider. In this model, the business person can form partnerships with farmers from various parts of the country,who will set up shop on the market and sell their fresh produce.
